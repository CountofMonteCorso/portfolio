Projects

What LinkedIn did for Facebook, Portflow.io is doing for Pinterest. Built in React, Portflow.io creates a professional social network designed to serve the needs of painters, artists, coders, and anyone who wants to post their work in a portfolio-style setting. Previously the problem with everyone having individual portfolio sites is that they might be hard to discover, but Portflow.io aims to connect people and make finding quality work easier through one central location.


My first project freelancing. The goal for MacroMeals was to make a site that could store user information on a database (MySQL) that would return their information to provide optimal meal plans based on macro nutrient needs and fitness goals utilizing a special calculator designed for that purpose. Additionally, it provides recommended recipes that can be favorited and saved on the user's profile or users can search recipes based on ingredients they have in their kitchen thanks to the Spoonacular API. MacroMeals would go on to become the inspiration for MacroKitchen.
