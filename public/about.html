Who is this guy?

Obviously at this point, you're aware that I make stuff online and that I love parallax (the scrolling animation) and space. In fact there's a reason I chose to use all pictures of nebulae specifically. Nebula are these big, beautiful interstellar clouds of dust, helium, hydrogen, and all kinds of gasses; they're not a star yet, but they contain all the stuff that you need to make stars. I see myself like the nebula: while I'm still in the very early stages of my new career, I've got all the tools I need to make some really cool things on the web.

But when I'm not on my computer coding away, you can find me all over town working out on gymnastic rings, training in mixed martial arts, relaxing with a good book/ movie/ video game, being the coolest uncle ever, or via the contact form on this website (I heard you like subtlety). Looking forward to working with you soon!
