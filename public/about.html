Who is this guy?

Obviously at this point, you're aware that I make stuff online and that I love parallax (the scrolling animation) and space. In fact there's a reason I chose to use all pictures of nebulae specifically. Nebula are these big, beautiful interstellar clouds of dust, helium, hydrogen, and all kinds of gasses; the stuff that you need to make stars. I see myself like the nebula: while I'm still in the very early stages of my new career, I've got all the tools I need to make the stars come to life for you and your business.

But when I'm not on my computer coding away, you can find me all over down, working out on gymnastic rings in the park, training in the mixed martial arts, relaxing with a good book or video game, working on my tan at the beach, or via the contact form on this website ;] Looking forward to working with you soon!
